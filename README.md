# MiniMarket Chain

## Project Title

MiniMarket Chain

## Project Description

MiniMarket Chain is a decentralized smart contract platform for managing products, inventory, and sales operations in a mini supermarket. Built using Soroban on the Stellar blockchain, it provides transparent inventory tracking, secure sales recording, and automated revenue management through immutable on-chain data.

The platform enables store owners to manage products and monitor business activities without relying on centralized databases, ensuring trust, transparency, and data integrity.

## Project Vision

The vision of MiniMarket Chain is to provide small retail stores and mini supermarkets with a decentralized, secure, and efficient management solution powered by blockchain technology.

By leveraging Soroban smart contracts, the platform ensures transparent inventory control, tamper-proof sales records, and reliable revenue tracking, helping businesses reduce operational risks while improving accountability and trust.




## Key Features

* **Product Management:** Store owners can add, update, and manage products.
* **Inventory Tracking:** Monitor product stock levels in real time.
* **Sales Processing:** Record sales transactions securely on-chain.
* **Revenue Management:** Automatically calculate and store total revenue.
* **Stock Validation:** Prevent sales when inventory is insufficient.
* **Immutable Records:** All inventory and sales activities are permanently stored on-chain.
* **Access Control:** Owner-restricted management functions to prevent unauthorized modifications.
* **Transparent Data:** Product information, inventory status, and revenue records can be queried transparently.

## Usage Instructions

1. **Initialize Contract:** Deploy the contract and assign the supermarket owner.
2. **Add Products:** Create products with unique IDs, names, prices, and initial stock quantities.
3. **Manage Inventory:** Update stock quantities whenever products are restocked.
4. **Process Sales:** Record product purchases and automatically reduce inventory.
5. **Track Revenue:** Monitor accumulated revenue generated from sales.
6. **Query Data:** Retrieve product information and revenue statistics at any time.

## Future Scope

* **Multi-Store Management:** Support multiple supermarkets within a single ecosystem.
* **Employee Roles:** Implement role-based permissions for managers and staff.
* **Sales Analytics:** Generate daily, weekly, and monthly sales reports.
* **Product Categories:** Organize products by category and department.
* **Customer Loyalty Programs:** Reward repeat customers with blockchain-based loyalty points.
* **Token Payments:** Integrate Stellar assets and Soroban token payments.
* **Purchase History:** Maintain detailed transaction histories for auditing.
* **Notification System:** Alert store owners about low inventory and sales milestones.
* **Dashboard Integration:** Build web and mobile dashboards for easier management.

## Technology Stack

* Rust and Soroban SDK for secure smart contract development.
* Stellar blockchain for decentralized and immutable data storage.
* Soroban persistent storage for inventory and transaction management.
* Cryptographic authentication for secure owner authorization.
